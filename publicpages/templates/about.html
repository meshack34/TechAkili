Our Story

TechAkili Technologies is on a mission to empower individuals and organizations through high-impact training and innovative software solutions.

Founding Background

TechAkili Technologies was founded by a group of Kenyan professionals based in the US, UK, Germany, and France, with a shared vision: to bridge the digital skills gap and empower individuals and organizations through cutting-edge technology and education. The founders, with extensive experience in technology, education, and social development, recognized the growing need for digital literacy in underserved regions.

Key Milestones

2020: The Beginning

The company was officially founded, with a primary focus on software development and digital skills training in Kenya.

2021: First Major Training Program

TechAkili launched its first comprehensive training program, providing over 500 professionals with critical digital skills.

2022: AI-Powered Learning Solutions

We introduced AI-driven learning solutions, helping businesses improve employee performance and engagement.

2023: Expanding Our Reach

TechAkili expanded its services to multiple African countries, reaching over 2,000 individuals with transformative digital skills training.

2024: International Partnerships & Growth

We formed strategic partnerships with international organizations, bringing high-quality education to thousands globally.

2025: Launch of Online Academy

We officially launched our online academy, making learning accessible to a global audience with cutting-edge courses.

Future Aspirations

Looking ahead, we have ambitious goals to further our impact, expand globally, develop AI-driven solutions, and continue our mission of community empowerment through education and technology.

  • Global expansion into Asia, Latin America, and the Middle East.
  • Strategic partnerships for sustainable development and social change.
  • Innovative AI-powered learning tools for personalized education.
  • Community-driven impact projects for digital entrepreneurship and social change.
  • Advanced software solutions for businesses to streamline digital transformation.
